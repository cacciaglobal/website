Services

Scaffolding

Highest quality scaffolding solutions chosen for their safety specifications.

At Caccia Global we mainly use Layher scaffolding imported from Germany, carefully chosen for its safety specifications, ease of use and ability to build scaffold on a grand scale. At Caccia Global we use engineers to ensure our builds meet the highest safety standards.

For smaller builds on tight frames, why not use our Intaks system; an Indian designed scaffold system with a minimal footprint on the building, allowing multiple trades to work at once. With our skilled team, the Intaks system can be installed in approximately four hours for a standard house.

Propping

Depending on the application we will use either our Layher system scaffold or Acrow propping.

Our propping solutions high strength capacity provides a quick and simple method of temporary support of formwork and falsework for reinforced concrete floors and beams. It also provides raking shores to support formwork for columns, walls and staircases.

Our props can easily be adjusted to achieve the exact height requirement for your project. Our propping system accessory, U head adjustable jacks, LVL SG11 Propping timbers, Tilt propping base plates and ground anchors.

Safety Netting

Combine our scaffold and safety netting for a complete working safely at heights solution for commercial and residential projects.

When installing trusses safety nets are the most efficient option to keep your team safe. Our FASET trained safety netting installers are experienced installers. Caccia Global safety nets are electronically monitored to ensure they are fit for use and we conduct weekly checks to comply with Work Safe best practice. You will receive electronic handover documentation once our safety nets have been installed.

Combine our Caccia Global safety nets with our scaffold systems to complete your on site safety building solution.
