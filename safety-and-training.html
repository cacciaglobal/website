Safety & Training

Safety

Safety is the most important aspect of our Business. As one of the leaders in the scaffold industry, we provide a fully trained and highly experienced workforce who are capable of dealing with any scaffolding requirement in a Safe and efficient manner. Our supervisory team continually receive extensive hands-on training to ensure both our customerâ€™s and regulatory requirements are met. Each of our employees are issued with all the necessary items of Personal Protective Equipment that are required to carry out their work safely. We strive to maintain our enviable track Record for safety. never has, and will never, sacrifice safety for lower costs.

We produce detailed and job-specific (Method Statements and on-site Toolbox Talks On safety issues) to increase safety awareness of our scaffolding teams and the quality of our on-site service. We use the safe tag system. The assurance of quality is fundamental to all the work undertaken by us. We employ a high ratio of competent and experienced supervisors to manage our operatives including full-time supervisors for Emergency work. Safety procedure and policies are continually monitored and reviewed with a view to implement preventative action and improvement plans.
